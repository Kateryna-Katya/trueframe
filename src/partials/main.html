About The Game

Look, we wanted something that wouldn't put you to sleep but also wouldn't stress you out after work - something that relaxes you while still giving your brain a little workout.

Relax your mind and sharpen your brain with this addictive and fun tile-matching puzzle adventure. Every level throws you a different board setup, but the goal's always the same - just match 3 tiles and clear that board!

The controls are simple and intuitive. You only need to tap and match 3 identical tiles to remove them from the board. Sure, the rules are simple enough, but man, these puzzles can get pretty tough. You'll find yourself actually planning moves ahead instead of just tapping randomly.

The graphics are actually really cute - I'm obsessed with the little fruit tiles, they're so satisfying to match, plus some animal ones that are honestly adorable. Makes each level feel fresh and colorful. Combined with calm visuals and relaxing sounds , the game creates a soothing and stress-free environment.

Whether you’re a puzzle pro or a casual gamer, Tile Match 3 is the perfect game to relax and train your brain. With thousands of fun levels and new levels added regularly, the fun never ends.
